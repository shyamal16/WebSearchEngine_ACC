https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Template:POTD/2021-11-17 Template:POTD/2021-11-17 - Wikipedia Template:POTD/2021-11-17 From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia < Template:POTD Jump to navigation Jump to search Picture of the day archives: 2021 November < 2021 November 16 2021 November 18 > Picture of the day Georg Braun (1541 – 10 March 1622) was a Catholic cleric and topo-geographer from Cologne. From 1572 to 1617, he edited the Civitates orbis terrarum, which contains 546 prospects, bird's-eye views and maps of cities from around the world. This copper plate print was published in the first volume of the work; it was engraved by the Flemish mapmaker Frans Hogenberg and shows a historical view of Trier.Credit: Frans Hogenberg and Georg Braun Archive – More featured pictures...
