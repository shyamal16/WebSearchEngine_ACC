https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Template:POTD/2021-11-22 Template:POTD/2021-11-22 - Wikipedia Template:POTD/2021-11-22 From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia < Template:POTD Jump to navigation Jump to search Picture of the day archives: 2021 November < 2021 November 21 2021 November 23 > Picture of the day Baby Huwae (22 November 1939 – 5 June 1989) was an Indonesian model, film actress and singer. Born in Rotterdam, Netherlands, she moved to Indonesia, where she took up modelling, by the 1950s. She entered the film industry in 1958, and gained popularity following the success of Asrama Dara. Over the next few years, Huwae acted in a further five films and established a girl group, the Baby Dolls, with several actresses who had appeared in her second film. She made a guest appearance in one more film in 1971 after a ten-year hiatus.Photograph credit: Tati Photo Studio; restored by Chris Woodrich Archive – More featured pictures...
